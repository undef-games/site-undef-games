**Effective date:** June 28, 2026

undef games is operated by **MindTenet&trade; LLC** ("we", "us"), registered at
8225 SW Cirrus Dr, Beaverton, OR 97008, United States. This policy explains what
we collect, why, and your choices.

## Information we collect

- **Account and contact information** you give us when you sign up or get in
  touch — such as your email address, account name, and anything you include in a
  message.
- **Payment information** when you buy something. Payments are handled by
  third-party processors — currently **Stripe** and **Square** (we may add
  others). We never see or store your full card or bank-account number; these
  processors handle payment data as PCI-DSS service providers and return only a
  token and limited transaction metadata (such as the last four digits and card
  brand).
- **Usage and device data** (such as pages visited and IP address) processed by
  our hosting and CDN provider, **Cloudflare**, and used for analytics to
  understand how the site and our games are used.

## How we use information

To run your account, provide and improve our games and tools, process purchases,
prevent fraud, meet legal obligations, and support you. We do not sell your
personal information.

## Who we share it with

Only with service providers who help us operate: our **payment processors**
(Stripe and Square) and our hosting/CDN provider (**Cloudflare**). Each processes
data on our behalf under its own terms.

## Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to run the site, and analytics cookies to
understand how it's used. You can control or block cookies through your browser
settings; blocking some may affect how the site works.

## Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above. We
retain transaction and financial records for as long as required by law — up to
13 years — and delete or anonymize other personal information once it's no longer
needed.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or
port your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise
them, contact us below.

## International transfers & children

Our services are operated from the United States (Oregon). If you access them
from elsewhere, you consent to processing your information in the United States.
Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly
collect their data.
