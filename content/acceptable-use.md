**Effective date:** June 28, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy applies to everyone who uses undef games (operated by
**MindTenet&trade; LLC**) — buyers, players, creators, and developers. It is part
of our [Terms of Service](/terms/). Breaking these rules can lead to content
removal, loss of access, suspension, or termination, and where appropriate, we may
report activity to the authorities.

## Don't

- **Break the law** or infringe anyone's intellectual-property, privacy, or other
  rights, or upload content you don't have the rights to.
- **Upload harmful code** or attack the Services — malware, exploits, denial of
  service, or attempts to gain unauthorized access.
- **Cheat or manipulate** — exploit bugs, use unauthorized automation or bots,
  manipulate games, wallets, currency, ratings, reviews, or payouts, or interfere
  with other players.
- **Circumvent protections** — bypass, remove, or defeat DRM, content protection,
  watermarks, rate limits, or security measures.
- **Abuse others** — harass, threaten, defame, or promote hate or violence, or
  impersonate others.
- **Post prohibited content** — anything unlawful; sexual content involving minors
  (which we report); content that facilitates fraud or serious harm.
- **Spam or scrape** — send bulk unsolicited messages, or harvest data or content
  from the Services except as expressly permitted.
- **Misuse the marketplace or APIs** — list infringing or misdescribed products,
  launder funds, evade fees, or use developer access to extract our data or build
  a competing copy.

## Reporting

To report a violation, email **[support@undef.games](mailto:support@undef.games)**.
For copyright specifically, see our [Copyright / DMCA Policy](/dmca/).

## Enforcement

We may investigate and take any action we consider appropriate, including removing
content, limiting features, reversing transactions or payouts, and suspending or
terminating accounts. We aim to be fair and proportionate, but we prioritize the
safety and integrity of the Services and their users.
