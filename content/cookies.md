This policy explains how undef games, operated by **MindTenet&trade; LLC**, uses
cookies and similar technologies. It supplements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).

## What cookies are

Cookies are small files a site stores on your device. We also use similar
technologies such as local storage. Together we call them "cookies."

## The cookies we use

- **Strictly necessary** — required to run the site and keep you securely signed
  in (session, authentication, and security/anti-fraud). These can't be turned off
  without breaking core features.
- **Preferences** — remember choices such as theme (light/dark).
- **Analytics** — help us understand how the Services are used so we can improve
  them. These are provided through our infrastructure provider, **Cloudflare**.

We do **not** use third-party advertising cookies or sell your data.

## Managing cookies

You can control or block cookies through your browser settings, and delete cookies
already stored. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may stop parts of the Services
from working (for example, staying signed in).
