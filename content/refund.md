**Effective date:** June 28, 2026

This policy covers purchases from undef games, operated by **MindTenet&trade;
LLC**. Payments are handled by third-party processors — currently **Stripe** and
**Square** (we may add others). Refunds are issued to your original payment method
through the processor used for the purchase. What you're eligible for depends on
the type of product.

## Digital goods and print-on-demand

All sales of digital products (games, tools, downloadable content) and
print-on-demand items are **final** once the item is delivered or sent for
production — except where it is faulty, materially not as described, or was not
delivered, or where the law requires a refund.

## Physical goods

For physical products we stock, refund and return eligibility is shown on the
product listing. Where a return is offered, request it within the window stated
there, with the item unused and in its original condition. Unless stated
otherwise on the listing, sales are final.

## Subscriptions and cancellations

Subscriptions renew automatically at the stated interval until you cancel. You can
cancel any time — from your account or by emailing us — and cancellation takes
effect at the end of the current billing period; you keep access until then. We
don't refund partial periods unless the law requires it. We'll give reasonable
notice before any change to a subscription price, and a failed payment may lead to
suspension or cancellation.

## How to request a refund

Email **[support@undef.games](mailto:support@undef.games)** with your order
details and the reason. We aim to respond within a few business days.

## Disputes & chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us first — we can usually
resolve it faster than a bank dispute. You retain the right to dispute a charge
with your card issuer; chargebacks are handled through the relevant payment
processor.
