**Effective date:** June 28, 2026

This policy covers purchases on undef games, operated by **MindTenet&trade; LLC**.
Payments and refunds are processed through **Stripe** (we may add other
processors). How it works:

- **Your statutory rights always apply** and are never reduced by this policy.
- **undef games guarantees a minimum standard** (below). No product on the
  marketplace may offer worse than this.
- **Sellers may offer more generous terms.** Each product shows the refund terms
  that apply to it; where a seller's terms are more generous than our minimum,
  the seller's terms apply.

## Our minimum guarantee

**Digital products (games, tools, downloadable content).** You may request a
refund within **14 days** of purchase if the product hasn't been substantially
downloaded or used, or if it's faulty, materially not as described, or wasn't
delivered — whichever gives you the better outcome. Our own first-party products
go further with a **30-day money-back guarantee**, even if downloaded and tried,
subject to anti-abuse review.

**Physical products.** Refund and return eligibility is shown on the product
listing. Where a return is offered, request it within the stated window with the
item unused and in its original condition. Personalized or made-to-order items
(such as engraved or custom-printed goods) are non-refundable once production has
started, except where faulty or not as described.

**Wallet top-ups and virtual currency.** Unspent wallet top-ups may be refunded
within 14 days. **Once virtual currency (such as tokens or cycles) has been spent,
it is non-refundable**, except where the law requires. Virtual currency has no
cash value and cannot be cashed out.

**Bundles and packs.** A bundle is refunded as a whole (we can't split it); the
entire bundle and its entitlements are removed on refund.

**Pre-orders and early access.** Pre-orders can be cancelled and refunded any time
before release and for 14 days after release. Early-access purchases follow the
digital-products rule above.

## How refunds are issued

Approved refunds go back to your **original payment method**, or — if you prefer
and it's offered — as **wallet credit** (wallet credit is usually faster; a cash
refund can take a few business days to appear). Refunding a purchase revokes the
related entitlement.

## Subscriptions and cancellations

Subscriptions renew automatically until you cancel. You can cancel any time from
your account; cancellation takes effect at the **end of the current billing
period**, and you keep access until then. We don't refund partial periods unless
the law or the seller's terms require it. We give reasonable notice before a
subscription price change.

## How to request a refund

Request a refund from your account, or email
**[support@undef.games](mailto:support@undef.games)** with your order details and
the reason. We aim to respond within a few business days. For seller products, we
may involve the seller in the decision consistent with our minimum guarantee.

## Anti-abuse

Refunds are meant to protect buyers, not to be a way to use products for free. We
monitor for abuse and may decline refunds in individual cases where the right is
being misused, to the extent the law allows.

## Disputes and chargebacks

If a charge looks wrong, please contact us first — we can usually resolve it
faster than a bank dispute. You keep the right to dispute a charge with your card
issuer; note that a chargeback typically results in the related entitlement being
revoked. Chargebacks are handled through the relevant payment processor.
